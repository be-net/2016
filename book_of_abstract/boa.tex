\chapter*{Introduction}

The 6th edition of the Belgian Network Research Meeting (BeNet) will be held
at the Université catholique de Louvain (UCL) on December 15th, 2016.

This annual meeting is a place for the Belgium-based researchers interested in the network paradigm to meet and communicate their results, regardless of the discipline: biology, communication, economics, geography, history, informatics, mathematics, medicine, physics, sociology, statistics, etc.

Previous editions were held in:
Namur (UNamur 2015),
Brussels (ULB 2014),
Leuven (IIS/KUL 2013),
Antwerpen (UA 2012) and
Brussels (VUB 2011).

The scope of the meeting includes but is not restricted to:

\begin{itemize}
  \item  Social \& organizational theory
  \item  Economics \& Finance
  \item  Social media \& On-line activities
  \item  Human communication \& mobility
  \item  Information diffusion \& opinion dynamics
  \item  Political networks \& collective action
  \item  Ecology, Ethology, food webs
  \item  Biology \& Bioinformatics
  \item  Epidemiology
  \item  Physics, Mathematics \& Statistics
  \item  Modelling \& Data Analysis
  \item  Geography \& Urban Dynamics
\end{itemize}
